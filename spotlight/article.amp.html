Taketei

SEAN YOONG | MONDAY , 11 DEC 2017

Taman Desa's latest spot for Japanese soul food mainly tackles ramen - the Jiro Ramen (RM16.00++; add RM5.30++ for chashu) might not exactly be the massive bowl with a mound of pork shoulder meat, cabbage and bean sprouts like its Tokyo inspiration, but it's reasonably substantial and satisfactory enough for its price.

Worth sharing: The Okonomiyaki Gyoza (RM16++), an indulgently tasty hybrid of two Japanese staples.
